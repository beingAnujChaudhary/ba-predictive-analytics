# Business Recommendations

## Executive Summary

The analysis demonstrates that customer behaviour can be predicted with machine learning techniques, providing opportunities to improve operational efficiency and marketing effectiveness.

---

# Recommendation 1

## Personalized Marketing Campaigns

Target customers based on predicted booking probability instead of mass marketing campaigns.

Expected Benefits:

- Higher conversion rates
- Lower acquisition costs
- Better customer experience

---

# Recommendation 2

## Early Customer Engagement

Customers with shorter purchase lead times exhibit stronger booking behaviour.

Develop personalized offers before travel dates.

---

# Recommendation 3

## Promote Ancillary Services

Customers purchasing additional services such as baggage, preferred seating, and in-flight meals demonstrate higher engagement.

Bundle ancillary products into promotional campaigns.

---

# Recommendation 4

## Route-Based Marketing

Booking behaviour differs across routes and destinations.

Develop region-specific marketing strategies.

---

# Recommendation 5

## Dynamic Customer Segmentation

Integrate the predictive model into CRM systems to automatically classify customers into:

- High Conversion Probability
- Medium Conversion Probability
- Low Conversion Probability

This enables more efficient allocation of marketing resources.

---

# Recommendation 6

## Continuous Model Monitoring

Customer behaviour changes over time.

Retrain the predictive model periodically using newly collected booking data.

---

# Future Improvements
